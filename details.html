Sanctuary Craft & Details

Details regarding our culinary and beverage preparation parameters, sourcing methods, and hosting structures.

Sourdough Pizza Craft

Our pizza dough undergoes a slow 48-hour cold fermentation process using wild yeast cultures. Baked in stone-hearth deck ovens at exactly 450°C to create a charred, crisp crust with a light interior crumb.

Water Mineralization

Water used in coffee extraction and dough is filtered via reverse osmosis and enriched with magnesium and calcium. We target an exact mineral density of 120 TDS, poured at precise temperatures (e.g. 91°C).

Smoke & Salt Bakeries

We reject refined sugar in favor of organic, unrefined jaggery sourced from Maharashtra. Our cookies are finished with sea salt harvested from the Konkan coast, cold-smoked in our micro-studio.

Narrative Hosting

Every guest order is accompanied by a heavy-stock paper Provenance Card. These cards document the farm location, altitude, processing, and craftsmanship behind each ingredient served.
