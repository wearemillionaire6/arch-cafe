Chapter II

Artisanal Sourdough Pizza

Hand-stretched slow-rise 48-hour sourdough crusts baked in our 450°C stone-hearth deck ovens. Crispy exterior, airy interior crumb.

01
Classic Italian DOC

ARCH • Margherita DOC

A study in simplicity. Sweet Italian San Marzano tomato passata, melted buffalo mozzarella pearls, fresh torn green basil leaves, and a generous drizzle of cold-pressed extra virgin olive oil over our 48-hour slow-fermentation crust.

Origin: Campania tomato // SoBo Crust Crust: 48hr Sourdough Temperature: Baked at 450°C
02
American-Italian Fusion

ARCH • Hot Honey & Soppressata

Spicy Calabrian soppressata salami, bubbly fresh mozzarella, and parmigiano reggiano, finished with a generous post-bake drizzle of organic wild honey from Maharashtra infused with red chilis.

Origin: Calabria Salami // Maharashtra Honey Crust: 48hr Sourdough Profile: Sweet & Spicy
03
Italian White Pizza

ARCH • Quattro Formaggi Bianca

A white base study of four premium cheeses: blue gorgonzola dolce, taleggio, buffalo mozzarella, and aged parmigiano reggiano. Finished with fresh rosemary needles and cracked black pepper.

Origin: Northern Italy Cheeses Base: Bianca (No Tomato) Herb: Graham Road Rosemary
04
Modern American Gourmet

ARCH • Truffle Mushroom & Arugula

Sautéed wild forest porcini and button mushrooms, creamy goat cheese, mozzarella, and a drizzle of rich white truffle oil. Finished with fresh peppery baby arugula tossed in lemon juice.

Origin: Goan Porcini // Umbrian Truffle Greens: Baby Arugula Profile: Rich & Earthy
